Clipz is an app to share your gaming clips.

It may be yours plays, from your friends or even an amazing or funny play from someone you played with!

Clipz is a project created in Zero To Mastery Angular course. In the course I learned the latest features in Angular like Routing, Directives, Pipes, Services, Ahead of Time & Just in Time Compilation. Also about Angular Design Patterns and how to deal with Memory Leaks in my projects to improve their performance. The course taught to use WebAssembly, Rust, Rxjs and Streams in my applications to boost performance and combine with the power of Angular.
